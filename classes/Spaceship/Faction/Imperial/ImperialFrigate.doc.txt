
<- ImperialFrigate ------------------------------------------------------------
The ImperialFrigate class is a Spaceship that is part of the Imperial faction.

Usage:
new ImperialFrigate( $x, $y );
x and y are the coordinates of the spaceship.

Size: 1x4 cases
Hull points: 5
PP: 10
Speed: 15
Handling: 4
Shield: 0
Weapons: Side laser batteries

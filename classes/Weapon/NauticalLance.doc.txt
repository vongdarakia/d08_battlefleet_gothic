
<- NauticalLance --------------------------------------------------------------
The NauticalLance class is a type of Weapon.

Usage:
new ImperialDestroyer();

Charges: 0
Short Range: 1 to 30 cells
Middle Range: 31 to 60 cells
Long Range: 61 to 90 cells
Effect Zone: A straight line or column 1 cell wide that start from the front of the
ship.
